Goals and Purpose

The mission of the Chamber of Commerce of American Falls is to bring together local businesses to promote the wonderful place that we call home. To help voluntarily unite, promote, and support our areas businesses and business developements, and enrichment. Fostering the unity of our citizens and cultures. Our current goals are as follows:

  1. Increase our membership by 10 businesses and or individuals.
  2. Increase exposure and promotions of town wide events that host local vendors, like AF Days.
  3. Promoting our city by developing and implementing different programs to attractnew business or businesses to our city.
