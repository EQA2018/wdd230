Instructions

Please complete the form below to request a membership into the American Falls Chamber of Commerce. Any and every lable that is marked with a red-dashed cell is required to be completed and filled. The required information is: your first and last name, business name, email, and membership level. When the required cell is filled with valid information, it will turn green.

Below is a list of our membership levels and information about each one.

  • Gold Membership Benefits:
    • Cost: $1,000 per year
    • 80% discount on tickets to the annual conference
    • 50% discount on luncheon tickets.
    • All benefits in lower membership levels included.
  • Silver Membership Benefits:
    • Cost: $400 per year
    • 50% discount on table for AF Days and tickets to annual conference.
    • 5 business days of training for managers, employees, and owners per year.
    • All benefits in lower membership levels included.
  • Bronze Membership Benefits:
    • Cost: $200 per year
    • 25% discount on table for AF Days and tickets to annual conference.
    • Free social media advertisements.
    • All benefits in lower membership levels included.
  • Non-Profit Membership Benefits:
    • Cost: $100 per year
    • 50% discount on tickets to the annual conference.
    • 30% discount on table for AF Days.
    • Free social media advertisements.
