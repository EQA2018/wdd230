Annual Preston Pet Adoption

Preston's local animal shelters, animal foster parents, and more are all coming together to help find these wonderful animals their forever homes! From dogs to cats to fish, they are all here excited to meet you! Adoption fees are listed at each stand. All animals, other than thoses that are too young, are up to date on all vaccines, shots, and check ups. Those old enough have been fixed or spade.

With so many different options, finding an animal that matches with your personality and lifestyle is a possibility! Want a puppy or kitten that you can watch grow? Come on over! Want an animal that is still young but has some training? We have them! Want an animal that is completely trained and just looking for their forever home? They are here! So come on down to the Annual Preston Pet Adoption to find the pet that is meant for you!
