Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

Instagram

As you can see the text is surrounded by white space or "negative space". This helps draw the readers attention straight to it without any distractions. It emphasises the text and buttons whist still looking clean and organized.

Fitt's Law

Amazon

The biggest and easiest button to push is the sign in button. Its bright yellow color catches your eye and the white space around it helps it stand out even more. Also it is towards the top of the screen extending to almost either side. This making it easier to see and press.

Law of Proximity

Netflix

There are three grouping of text. The first line is bold and eye catching, the second simple and to the point. And the third hooks you in with questions and ease of completion. Eventhough there is spacing between them there proximity indicateds that they work together to get the message across.
