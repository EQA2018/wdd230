The site name will be Rocky Mountain Country Weather and Events with the domain of www.rockymountaincountryweatherandevents.com.

Site Purpose

Rocky Mountain Country Weather and Events was created to provide reliable and accurate information in regards to weather and events for Rexburg, Idaho Falls, and Pocatello areas. Community activities, latest weather predictions, news articles, upcomeing Local events, and more will all be found here to keep you upto date and ready for the day.
