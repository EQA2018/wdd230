Target Audience

College students, parents, and other adults who want to know what is going on and how the weather will effect their plans and or events.
Between the ages of 18-45. Living between Rexburg, Idaho Falls, Pocatello, and surrounding areas. Income between $40,000 to $75,000 (after college or in career).

Persona 1: Emily

Emily is a 23 year old college student who is new to the area. She likes to hike, bike, and be outdoors. Enjoys going watching movies, trying new things, and meeting new people. She comes from a large family and is not afraid to go out on her own to try new things. Currently she is getting her degree in nursing. Because she is new to the area she has stayed close to Rexburg, but is ready to go to different towns to learn and experience more. Her roommates enjoy going to a neiboring town for a farmers market and she is planning on going with them next time.

Persona 2: Emma

Emma is a mother of one 2 yeear old daughter. Her daughter, Sarah, loves everything outdoors, animals, and Coco Melon. She is very energetic and does not like to sit still. Emma is also going to school for her bachelors in early childhood development and is currently taking 3 classes. Between her energetic daughter, homework, zoom meetings, cooking, cleaning, and more she does not have much time for anything else. Knowing what activities are going on each day that will help her daughter release some energy without changing her outfit many times is her goal.

Scenarios

College students finishes their homework early and wants to celebrate. Feeling adventurous they want to go to a neiboring town. What activites or events are going on?

Mom of two small children is planning an outing to the Idaho Falls zoo or the movie theater. What is the weather going to be like that day? Outside weather or inside weather?

Winter is coming soon and FHE mom and dad are planning an FHE activity. They are wanting to do one last big activity but are unsure if the weather will hold. Looking for the weather predictions as well as local (indoor) activities helps them choose what to do and also gives them a back up plan if the weather predictions are wrong.

Are there any farmers markets? By looking at local communities and upcoming events three different farmers markets will be open on Friday. Shelly, Rexburg, and Idaho Falls.

Family is coming to see you and is asking what the weather will be like so they know what to pack. What should they pack?
